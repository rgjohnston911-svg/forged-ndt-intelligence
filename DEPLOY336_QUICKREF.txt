KEY INPUTS (for "assess" action):

GEOMETRY:
  D          Inside diameter (mm)
  tnom       Nominal wall thickness (mm)
  tmm        Minimum measured thickness (mm)
  c          Flaw half-length in longitudinal direction (mm)

MATERIAL & CODE:
  P          Design pressure (MPa)
  S          Allowable stress (MPa)
  E          Weld joint efficiency (0.0 to 1.0)
  W          Weld strength reduction factor (B31.3, default 1.0)

CORROSION & SERVICE:
  FCA        Future corrosion allowance (mm, default 0)
  corrosion_rate  mm/year (optional, for remaining life)
  service_class   "general" or "high_consequence" (default general)

OPTIONAL:
  component_type  "cylinder", "sphere", "pipe", "elbow", "nozzle"
  case_id         UUID for database tracking
  flaw_spacing    Spacing to adjacent flaw (for interaction check)
  grid_data       Array of {x, y, thickness} for CTP analysis

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

KEY OUTPUTS (from "assess" response):

REGULATORY DECISION METRICS:
  RSF         Remaining Strength Factor (the decision number)
  RSFa        Allowable RSF (0.9 general, 0.75 high-consequence)
  acceptance  "ACCEPTABLE" | "REDUCED_MAWP" | "REPAIR_REQUIRED"

THICKNESS & STRENGTH:
  tmin        Minimum required thickness per ASME/API
  Rt          Remaining thickness ratio
  lambda      Flaw length parameter
  Mt          Folias stress concentration factor

PRESSURE LIMITS:
  MAWP        Nominal Maximum Allowable Working Pressure
  MAWPr       Reduced MAWP (if RSF < RSFa)
  MAWP_reduction  % reduction from nominal

ASSET LIFE:
  remaining_life      Years until tmin reached (without FCA)
  remaining_life_fca  Years until tmin+FCA reached (with FCA)

INTERPRETIVE:
  summary                  Narrative assessment
  risk_characterization    LOW_RISK | MODERATE_RISK | HIGH_RISK
  recommendations          Action plan (operate, monitor, repair, etc.)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DECISION TREE:

IF acceptance == "ACCEPTABLE":
  → Component is fit-for-service as designed
  → Continue normal operation
  → Re-inspect per plant procedure (typically in 2-5 years)

IF acceptance == "REDUCED_MAWP":
  → Component acceptable ONLY at reduced pressure
  → Operating pressure must not exceed MAWPr
  → Schedule re-inspection in 6 months
  → Plan repair/replacement within 1-2 years

IF acceptance == "REPAIR_REQUIRED":
  → Component NOT fit-for-service
  → Immediate corrective action required
  → Options: depressurize, weld overlay, insert repair, replacement
  → Do not operate at normal pressure

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA (from API 579-1 Table 5.2):

RSF >= 0.9    [For General Service]       → ACCEPTABLE
RSF >= 0.75   [For High-Consequence]      → ACCEPTABLE
0.5 < RSF < threshold                      → REDUCED_MAWP (pressure limit applies)
RSF <= 0.5                                 → REPAIR_REQUIRED (urgent action)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMPONENT TYPES & TMIN FORMULAS:

CYLINDER (most common):
  tmin = (P × R) / (S × E - 0.6 × P)
  R = D/2

SPHERE:
  tmin = (P × R) / (2 × S × E - 0.2 × P)
  R = D/2

PIPE:
  tmin = (P × D) / (2 × (S × E × W + P × Y))
  Per ASME B31.3

ELBOW:
  tmin = (P × R) / (S × E × W - 0.6 × P)
  Branch connection stress factor

NOZZLE:
  tmin = (P × R) / (S × E × 0.8 - 0.6 × P)
  Reduced strength (0.8 factor)

TYPICAL ASSESSMENT WORKFLOW:

1. Collect field data:
   - Measure minimum wall thickness (UT, UT phased array)
   - Identify flaw dimensions from UT/phased array
   - Record design pressure, temperature
   - Obtain material spec from design drawings

2. Look up material properties:
   - S (allowable stress) - typically 165-250 MPa for carbon steel
   - E (weld joint efficiency) - 1.0 for seamless, 0.85-1.0 for welded
   - Corrosion rate - from historical inspection data or industry standard

3. POST to assess action with all inputs

4. Interpret response:
   - If ACCEPTABLE: document, schedule next inspection
   - If REDUCED_MAWP: implement pressure limit, schedule repair
   - If REPAIR_REQUIRED: initiate turnaround work order

5. Store in database (automatic via engine)

STRESS ALLOWABLE VALUES (Typical):

Carbon Steel SA-106B:
  Allowable stress S = 165-200 MPa (depending on temperature)

Stainless 304:
  Allowable stress S = 180-230 MPa

Carbon Moly (1.25Cr-0.5Mo):
  Allowable stress S = 195-245 MPa

Always verify against ASME Section VIII Div 1 Section II Part D for
the specific alloy at operating temperature.
