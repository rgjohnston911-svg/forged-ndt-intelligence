KEY TECHNICAL FEATURES
================================================================================

THINNING DAMAGE FACTOR TABLES:
  Embedded 4 lookup tables with 11 Art points each:
    - 0 Category A inspections: 1 to 1,200,000
    - 1 Category A inspection: ~1/10 reduction
    - 2 Category A inspections: ~1/50 reduction
    - 3 Category A inspections: ~1/200 reduction

  Interpolation algorithm for Art between table values using linear
  fractional weighting between lower/upper bounds.

FLUID CONSEQUENCE COEFFICIENTS:
  10 fluid categories with power-law model (CA = a × mass^b):
    C1-C2:  a=8.669,  b=0.98   (methane/ethane - jet fire)
    C3-C4:  a=55.13,  b=0.95   (propane/butane)
    C5:     a=96.53,  b=0.92   (pentane)
    C6-C8:  a=130.2,  b=0.90   (gasoline)
    C9-C12: a=103.4,  b=0.88   (diesel)
    C13-C16:a=79.94,  b=0.85   (gas oil)
    C17-C25:a=60.07,  b=0.82   (crude oil)
    H2:     a=16.47,  b=0.96   (hydrogen)
    H2S:    Toxic model (separate calculation)
    Steam:  Thermal model (separate calculation)

EQUIPMENT TYPES (8 base rates):
    Pipe:                3.06e-5 per meter per year
    Vessel:              7.56e-5 per item per year
    Tank bottom:         6.26e-4 per item per year
    Heat exchanger tube: 1.05e-3 per item per year
    Pump casing:         2.41e-4 per item per year
    Fired heater tube:   1.41e-4 per item per year
    Separator:           1.37e-4 per item per year
    Compressor casing:   1.05e-4 per item per year

INSPECTION EFFECTIVENESS CATEGORIES:
    A: POD ≥ 90% (Highly Effective)
    B: POD ≥ 70% (Usually Effective)
    C: POD ≥ 50% (Fairly Effective)
    D: POD ≥ 30% (Poorly Effective)
    E: POD < 30% (Ineffective)

MANAGEMENT SYSTEMS FACTOR SCALE:
    PSM 4.5+: 0.5  (Excellent program)
    PSM 3.0:  1.0  (Baseline)
    PSM 1.0:  1.75 (Poor program)
    Below 1.0: 2.0 (Missing/no program)
