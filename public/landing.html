Workflow

Field to Fitness in Four Steps

FORGED transforms raw inspection data into code-compliant decisions with a streamlined four-step workflow.

Capture

Enter case details through forms or voice. Specify asset, material, environment, and inspection findings.

Analyze

142+ engines evaluate damage mechanisms, check prerequisites, and compute Bayesian posteriors against your field evidence.

Adjudicate

Review ranked hypotheses, discriminating evidence, and code citations. Accept, override, or request deeper analysis.

Deliver

Generate audit-ready reports with full traceability. Every decision links back to the evidence and code authority.
